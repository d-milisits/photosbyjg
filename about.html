Hello, my name is Jonathan Greggo-- professional photographer based out of Whitehall, PA willing to travel where ever your moments are created.

When my hand isn't glued to a camera, I enjoy PC gaming, hiking, powerlifting, and spending time with my dogs. Once my first DSLR was purchased in 2017, I never looked back on this journey. Since then, photography has taken me to some of the most serene & beautiful places I would have never gotten the opportunity to visit. I have the utmost passion for the art of photography and am truly passionate about capturing those 1 of 1 moments and adding unique creative perspective in my work.

In the past, I have shot for major professional sport vendors for their own marketing and promotional purposes where I have learned how important it is to capture once in a lifetime moments. I offer professional business headshots, wedding and engagements, portraits, and sports. For me, there is no job too big or small and am looking forward to hearing from you!
